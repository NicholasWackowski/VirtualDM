Virtual Dungeon Mayhem

This is a student project created for the purpose of collecting data on different strategies of gameplay and incorporating it into an AI agent. The more games you play, the better our data is! Here's how you can help:

  1. Go to the 'Play Game' section of the navbar (or click here)
  2. Play the game at least 4 times (once as each different deck)
  3. After you have played enough games, take the "Big 5 Quiz" (here)
  4. [Only take the "Big 5 Quiz" once! Even if you play more games afterward, it skews our data if the same person retakes the quiz!]
  5. Feel free to continue playing more games! Or, consider buying the real product!
