Oriax the Clever (Rogue)

Description

Oriax the Clever is a thief and rogue, his deck based around clever use of tricks and deadly combos. Cards like Cunning Action, Stolen Potion, and Sneak Attack let you act many times in a single turn, playing many cards which deal damage. When playing against multiple opponents, Clever Disguise can allow you to 'hide' from danger, emulating the feeling of being a thief. Oriax can also use Pick Pocket to 'steal' cards from other players, playing them as your own.

Special Actions

  • Oriax1: Opponent cards can't affect you or your shields until your next turn.
  • Oriax2: All shields on the field are destroyed.
  • Oriax3: Play the top card of target player's deck.

Lia the Radiant (Paladin)

Description

Lia the Radiant is a paladin: a knight of justice. Her deck is a mix of healing, defense, and consistent offense. With cards like Divine Shield and Cure Wounds, she can keep having high health long into the game. Lia can also use cards like Divine Inspiration to regain cards she already played, while also healing damage.

Special Actions

  • Lia1: Choose one card in your discard pile and add it to your hand.
  • Lia2: Destroy all shields on the field.

Azzan the Mystic (Wizard)

Description

Azzan the Mystic is a wizard, and his deck is based around having all sorts of magical powers. Cards like Knowledge is Power and Mirror Image allow for having incredible defenses and hand sizes. Azzan's most special ability, however, is his necromancy magic "Vampiric Touch," which swaps your HP with an opponent's. With a single card, this allows you to switch from defeat to victory!

Special Actions

  • Azzan1: All players (including yourself) takes 3 damage.
  • Azzan2: Choose 1 'shield' card in play (controlled by an opponent). Now it belongs to yourself.
  • Azzan3: Swap HP between yourself and one opponent.

Sultha the Skullcrusher (Barbarian)

Description

Sultha Skullcrusher is a a barbarian, using great physical strength and constitution to deal and take damage. With cards like "Rage!" allow you to deal more damage with a single card than any other deck. With her pet dogs "Riff" and "Raff" acting as shields, she can also sustain a lot of damage.

Special Actions

  • Sultha1: Deal 1 damage to each opponent, heal 1 HP for each opponent.
  • Sultha2: All players discard their entire hand and draws 3 new cards.
  • Sultha3: Destroy all shields on the field.
